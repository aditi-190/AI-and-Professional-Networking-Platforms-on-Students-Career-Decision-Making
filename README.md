




# Final Research Title

### **The Influence of Generative AI-Assisted Career Guidance on Undergraduate Students' Career Decision-Making: The Mediating Roles of Career Exploration and Career Decision Self-Efficacy**

----------

# Research Aim

To investigate how undergraduate students' use of generative AI for career guidance influences their career decision-making, and to examine the mediating roles of career exploration and career decision self-efficacy.

----------

# Research Objectives

### Objective 1

To examine the extent to which undergraduate students use generative AI tools (e.g., ChatGPT, Gemini, Copilot) for career guidance.

### Objective 2

To investigate the relationship between generative AI-assisted career guidance and career exploration.

### Objective 3

To examine the relationship between career exploration and career decision self-efficacy.

### Objective 4

To determine the influence of career decision self-efficacy on students' career decision-making.

### Objective 5

To examine whether career exploration and career decision self-efficacy mediate the relationship between generative AI-assisted career guidance and career decision-making.

----------

# Research Questions

**RQ1:** How frequently do undergraduate students use generative AI for career guidance?

**RQ2:** Does the use of generative AI significantly influence career exploration?

**RQ3:** Does career exploration improve career decision self-efficacy?

**RQ4:** Does career decision self-efficacy improve career decision-making?

**RQ5:** Do career exploration and career decision self-efficacy mediate the relationship between AI-assisted career guidance and career decision-making?

----------

# Dataset Description

## Population

Undergraduate students enrolled in public and private universities.

----------

## Sample Size

-   Minimum: **300 students**
    
-   Recommended: **400–500 students**
    

This is sufficient for regression or Structural Equation Modeling (SEM).

----------

## Data Collection Method

-   Online questionnaire (Google Forms or Microsoft Forms)
    
-   Convenience and snowball sampling across multiple universities
    

----------

## Dataset Structure

Approximately **35–45 variables** divided into the following sections:

### Section A: Demographic Information

-   Age
    
-   Gender
    
-   University type
    
-   Department
    
-   Academic year
    
-   CGPA
    
-   Internship experience
    

----------

### Section B: Generative AI Usage (Independent Variable)

Measure students' use of AI tools such as ChatGPT, Gemini, Copilot, and Claude for:

-   Career advice
    
-   Resume writing
    
-   CV improvement
    
-   Interview preparation
    
-   Skill recommendations
    
-   Career exploration
    
-   Job search support
    

**Scale:** 5-point Likert scale

----------

### Section C: Career Exploration (Mediator 1)

Measure:

-   Career information seeking
    
-   Exploring different career options
    
-   Learning about required skills
    
-   Researching labour market trends
    
-   Seeking professional guidance
    

**Scale:** 5-point Likert scale

----------

### Section D: Career Decision Self-Efficacy (Mediator 2)

Measure students' confidence in their ability to:

-   Select a suitable career
    
-   Evaluate career alternatives
    
-   Set career goals
    
-   Solve career-related problems
    
-   Make informed career decisions
    

**Scale:** 5-point Likert scale

----------

### Section E: Career Decision-Making (Dependent Variable)

Measure:

-   Career decision confidence
    
-   Career decision clarity
    
-   Satisfaction with career choice
    
-   Readiness for career planning
    
-   Confidence in future career path
    

**Scale:** 5-point Likert scale
